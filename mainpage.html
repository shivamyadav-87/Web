ICONIC BRANDS YOU TRUST

TO MAKE YOUR HOME BETTER,

For more than 31 years MDPH has been innovating and launching best in class brands meeting

multiple consumer needs, spanning across various sectors.





Creating Future Together


We strive to create a better tomorrow
One that is marked by positive change and progress.


We at MDPH have built robust operations. We manufacture over 18 lac retail packs every day in our 4 manufacturing units, having a floor space of 10 lac sq ft, employing over 4000 labours with over 80% women. In India, we distribute over 1200+ SKUs through our network of 37 depots, 3500 distributors, and 7.5 lac plus retail outlets. We also export our products to over 40 countries with an overseas office in New York.









Moving forward with

Our Mission

MDPH remains committed to delivering high-quality,
reliable products and services to its customers, as we
strive to provide the best value possible.

INNOVATION

At MDPH, innovation is what we do best. Every aspect of our business is constantly reinvented by our start-up heart and global resources, Our efforts are fueled by a deep understanding of people - their needs, values, desires, and passions - which serves as a wellspring of inspiration for our innovative work.

By leveraging the reach and influence of our brands, we are committed to making a lasting, positive impact on the world and our business.

SUSTAINABLE GROWTH

We are committed to doing more good for our planet and society, not simply reducing harm. We believe in taking action on the social and environmental issues facing the world today, and we strive to enhance people's lives through the use of innovative, sustainable, and high-quality products. Our dedication to sustainability is a core part of our mission and we are constantly exploring new ways to minimize our impact on the environment while also making a positive impact on society. We believe that by taking a holistic approach to business, we can create a better future for everyone.
